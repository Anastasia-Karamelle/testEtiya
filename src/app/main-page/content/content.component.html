Delivery for online stores

For Customers who trade online throughout Ukraine, Nova Poshta offers delivery services to the buyer.

 Use of services of delivery of goods in branch or at the address at special rates
Automatic return of goods after 5 days of storage in the office

Ability to automate processes of registration and control of deliveries by means of the Personal account and API-functions.

To start cooperation you need:

  • Sign an agreement with a representative of the company "Mail";
  • After registration of the documents you can send your goods on behalf of your organization / company;
  • If you refuse to sign the contract, please register on our site through the tab "I - Business".
